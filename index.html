Frequently Asked Questions

Still wondering how it works? Start here.

Do you offer virtual tax preparation?

Yes. You can upload your documents securely and meet with us by phone or video. We’ll review your return together before filing.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the complexity of your return. We’ll provide a clear estimate up front so there are no surprises.

What do I need to bring to my appointment?

Typical documents include W-2s, 1099s, tax forms for interest, retirement, or investments, and your prior-year return. We’ll send you a checklist when you schedule.

Can you help if I receive a letter from the IRS?

Yes. Bring or upload the notice and we’ll review it with you to help you understand what it means and what options you may have.
